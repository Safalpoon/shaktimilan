Shakti Milan Samaj

THE ORGANIZATION FORMED, GOVERNED, LED AND DEFINED BY WOMEN LIVING WITH HIV

MISSION

Empowering women living with HIV through comprehensive support, advocacy, and community building initiatives.

VISION

A society where women living with HIV are empowered, respected, and have equal opportunities to thrive.

GOAL

To create sustainable support systems and eliminate stigma associated with HIV/AIDS in our communities.
