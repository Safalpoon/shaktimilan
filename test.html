Shakti Milan Samaj

Creating sustainable change and empowering communities around the world.

Shakti Milan Samaj (SMS) is an apolitical, non-profit organization based in Kathmandu, Nepal started by three HIV positive women who were trafficked and rescued in 1996, came together with a common mission to support and work for the empowerment of the trafficked HIV infected women and their children infected and affected by HIV.

Restoring Dignity, Fighting Stigma, and Uplifting Lives
